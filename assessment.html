EduBot

EduBot is an easy-to-access child-friendly web service. Our aim at EduBot is to help young children to asses there strengths and weaknesses, so that they can work on them and excel in academics.

Children can answer our assesment questions and review our analysis. There are three difficulty levels: easy, medium, and hard. Based on the results of the assesment and difficulty level of the questions, we generate feedback.

We built EduBot as a tool for students, and we hope that it is fruitful in development of many students.
